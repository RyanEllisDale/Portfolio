    About Turn Based

    Building on my experience from the third-person project, this university assignment allowed me to deepen my skills in several key areas. I gained experience in controlling the main camera actor, creating collectibles with a pseudo-inventory system, and establishing a turn order for actors, each derived from the "TurnActor" class. The turn order manager specifically targets the TurnActor Blueprint Class and its children, enabling any object to participate in the turn system. Using this setup, I was able to design moving walls, cover, and interactable obstacles that take their turn only when activated, enhancing the dynamic nature of the gameplay.

    What I learnt:

    This project introduced a new style of game for me, requiring significantly more research than my previous projects. It proved to be the most challenging, particularly due to the rigidities I faced with the camera and turn order systems. Despite these struggles, I’m extremely proud to have overcome these challenges and successfully brought the project to completion.

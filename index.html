About Me

I am a dedicated university student pursuing a degree in Games Programming at Teesside University. My journey in game development began in secondary school with Scratch, and I have since advanced to working with Unreal Engine and C++. With a background in games development and computer science, I have also assisted in teaching programming to fellow students. This portfolio highlights my technical skills, showcases my projects, and includes my education, contact information, and social media profiles, along with my CV.
